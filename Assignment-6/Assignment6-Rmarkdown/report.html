Is there a surge of bankruptcies due to Covid-19?

Helle Sandsdalen

8 10 2020

The purpose of this paper is to see if covid-19 caused a higher rate of bankruptcies in 2020 compared to the same months in 2019. The data is gathered from https://w2.brreg.no/kunngjoring/. The result is divided such that youll see the result for each county in Norway.

Cumulative number of bankruptcies in counties in Norway in 2019 and 2020

The grapsh above shows the cumulative number of bankruptcies per month in a year and per county. It shows all months in 2019 compared to the months from January to September in 2019. From the graphs it is possible to see that covid-19 have not had any immediately impact on number of bankruptcies during 2020. Although we dont see any significant raise in bankruptcies so far, the effect from covid-19 may not come until 2021. It will be interesting to see if we see a significant raise in bankruptcies for the next half year.
